ABOUT US

Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy

Our journey began at the Government Industrial Training Institute in Tiruvannamalai since 2013, supported by the government's provision of land for practice. From these humble origins, our classes have evolved into an Indoor Academy proudly known as Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy. It is with great pride that we declare ourselves as first indoor class in Tiruvannamalai district. Our commitment to excellence and innovation continues to shape the martial arts experience we offer. Join us as we celebrate the rich history and progress of Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy.

KARATE

Welcome to Martial Arts Sports Academy! Since 2013, our Government ITI classes in Thiruvannamalai, led by Renshi Veka Nagarajan, have thrived. As a Judge in the Asian Karate Federation and Chairman of the Referee Commission at TDSKA-Tiruvannamalai District Sports Karate-Do Association, he's elevated our program to an Indoor Academy. Inclusivity is key, with female coaches enhancing our diverse learning environment. Join us for a transformative martial arts experience!


SILAMBAM

Welcome to Martial Arts Sports Academy! Established in 2013 at Government ITI in Tiruvannamalai, we proudly offer Silambam classes under the guidance of Aasaan Veka Nagarajan. He holds a role as the Tamil Nadu Secretary of the Dhrona Traditional Silambam Sports Association. Affiliated with the Silambam Association of India, he has played a pivotal role in upgrading our sports program to an Indoor Academy. Join us for an enriching experience at Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy!


ARCHERY

Welcome to Martial Arts Sports Academy! Since 2013, located at Government ITI in Tiruvannamalai, we take pride in offering archery classes under the guidance of Mr. Veka Nagarajan. As a distinguished member of the Royal Archery for All association in Tamil Nadu, he has been instrumental in upgrading our sports program to an Indoor Academy. Join us for an exceptional experience at Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy!


FITNESS

Welcome to Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy! Commencing in September 2018 and instructed by Mr. Veka Nagarajan, our fitness classes cater to specific needs, with a focus on weight loss and workouts designed to alleviate symptoms of PCOD. Our upgraded sports program has transformed into an Indoor Academy, known as Vekan Martial Arts Academy. Emphasizing inclusivity, we are proud to have female coaches available.


YOGA

Welcome to Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy! Established in September 2018 and led by Mr. Veka Nagarajan, our academy has evolved into an Indoor Academy, elevating sports education. Mr.Vega Nagarajan, an M.Sc. in Yoga from Annamalai University, draws inspiration from his training with Yogi Vasanth Kumar in Kerala. Our commitment to diversity is reflected in the availability of female coaches. Join us for a holistic and enriching experience at Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy!


KIDS & WOMEN'S DEFENSE

Welcome to Vekan Martial Arts Sports Academy! Operating since 2013 at Government ITI in Thiruvannamalai, our dedicated Sensai, Renshi Veka Nagarajan, specializes in teaching self-defense for women and kids. With a background as a judge in the Asian Karate Federation and Referee Commission Chairman at TDSKA-Tiruvannamalai District Sports Karate-Do Association, he has transformed our sports program into an Indoor Academy. Proudly named Vekan Martial Arts Academy, we prioritize inclusivity with the presence of female coaches. Join us for a comprehensive and empowering martial arts experience!
